Plan a Visit

Acadia is famous for its stunning fall foliage, but it is also beautiful in the winter, when it is often blanketed in white. Winter scenic drives and hiking are thus popular options. Cross-country skiing and snowshoeing are wonderful ways to see the park in winter.

Plan Your Visit

There is no same-day availability for any park campsite. An online reservation is required to enter park campgrounds.

Peak season traffic can make it difficult to drive and park in Acadia. It also can trigger serious public safety hazards.
